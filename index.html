About U.S.T




This institution was established in March 1995 with the ratification of the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research and it is considered as a distinct continuation of the great contribution in the process of higher education in Sudan by developing modern and advanced curricula. The university aimed behind all this to give the labor force in Sudan the means of modernity through the following:

  • Training and qualifying students to the level of degree.
  • To contribute sufficiently to the enlightenment and development of society.
  • Documenting scientific and cultural relations with universities and higher institutes inside and outside the country, and the University by its diligent efforts is looking forward to the future with full awareness and this can only be achieved through the realization and perception of modern sciences and advanced technologies.
